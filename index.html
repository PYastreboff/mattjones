Hip

The hip is a ball-and-socket joint that connects the pelvis and the femur. It is responsible for weight-bearing, stability, and a wide range of leg movements. The hip joint is surrounded by muscles, tendons, and ligaments that support and protect it.

Knee

The knee is a complex hinge joint that connects the thigh bone and shin bone, allowing for flexion and extension of the leg. It supports the body weight during standing, walking, and running, and absorbs shock during movement.

Trauma

Recovery from physical trauma can be a long and difficult process, involving physical therapy, rehabilitation, and psychological support. Proper prevention measures, such as following safety guidelines, can help reduce the risk of physical trauma.
